HOUSE RULES & AGREEMENT:
1. Quiet Hours: Sunday to Thursday from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Friday and Saturday from 11:30 PM to 9:00 AM.
2. Guests: Roommates may host overnight guests for up to 3 consecutive days. Any longer stay requires prior written consent from all other roommates.
3. Shared Expenses: All shared expenses (utilities, internet, toilet paper) must be logged and split equally. Balances should be settled by the 5th of each month.
4. Chores: Each roommate is assigned chores weekly. Common areas (kitchen, living room) must be kept tidy. Common dishes must be washed within 24 hours.
5. Cleanliness: A roommate is responsible for cleaning up after their guests immediately.
